About Me

I'm Aditi — the kind of person who speaks less but feels a lot. While conversations tire me, writing revives me.

Writing isn’t planned; it just happens. Sometimes it’s a midnight rant, other times a quiet thought that needed somewhere to live.

This little space? It’s not curated, it’s real. It’s raw. It’s where my mind and thoughts finally find a home.
